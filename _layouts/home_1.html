LIFFT-Cashew

This is a 6-year program (2017-2023) funded by USDA to enhance the production and trade of cashews in local and international markets. The target areas are the cashew growing regions of Senegal, The Gambia and Guinea-Bissau.

The project works around three objectives which are:

  • Increase or maintain quality of RCN outturn in 52-54 lbs;
  • Strengthen cooperatives and sale up to 50% by organizing sales;
  • Increase processing of RCN to additional 30000 tons locally.
Read More

Four components working in perfect synergy Infrastructure: More than 130 kilometers of roads built Market Access: Organizing collective sales and commercialization Access to finance: Facilitating private investment and loans On-Farm Practices: Improving the quality of cashews and introducing organic certification
